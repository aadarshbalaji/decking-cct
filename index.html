Fraud ML Autodesk e-commerce

Fraud detection framework for a global design software platform

Reduced risk across a high-volume store by pairing behavioral data with resilient model design.

Problem

High-risk transactions and limited visibility into fraudulent patterns across multiple payment flows.

What we built

  • Feature-engineered behavioral and transactional signals (Python, SQL).

Market Intel Antora Energy

Data-driven market entry for cleantech heat batteries

Built a thermal load database and prioritization rubric to unlock $2B+ in European and APAC industrial opportunities.

Problem

Identify where to expand first across 200k+ industrial records with varying data quality.

What we built

  • Aggregated EEA and Erhart datasets (300k+ rows) into a clean, filterable model.
  • Analyzed 8 years of energy usage across 11k facilities; narrowed to top 500 then 100 high-fit sites.
  • Visualized thermal load shifts to prioritize pulp, fertilizer, chemical, and paper sectors.

Impact

Surfaced Western Australia, Victoria, and Nordics as highest-yield regions; enabled data-backed sequencing for sales and partnerships.
