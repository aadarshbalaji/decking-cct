Case Study · Antora Energy

Data-Driven Market Entry for Industrial Heat Batteries

How a Berkeley engineering team filtered 300k+ industrial records to pinpoint where to launch next.

Berkeley Consulting Collective · Product, Data, Delivery

Client Snapshot

Who

Antora Energy builds thermal batteries that store excess solar and wind energy to power industrial facilities.

Goal

Assess European and APAC market entry, using data to target the best regions and sectors for expansion.

Problem & Stakes

Challenge

Decide where to expand first across hundreds of thousands of industrial sites with uneven data quality.

What Was Needed

  • Consolidated, trustworthy dataset across multiple European and APAC sources.
  • Filterable rubric tied to thermal load and processing constraints.
  • Clear, defensible short list for sales and partnerships.

Data Ingestion

Sources

  • EEA Database (200,000+ data points).
  • Erhart Database (100,000+ data points).

Filtering Signals

  • Thermal Load — how much heat an HVAC system can process and sustain.
  • Maximum Processing Temperature — suitability for Antora’s battery tech.
  • Policy and incentive relevance by region (EU deals, country legislation, tax cuts).

Analysis & Narrowing

Process

  • Standardized 300k+ rows, focusing on thermal load and processing temperature.
  • Modeled 8 years of energy usage across 11,000 industrial facilities.
  • Weighted rubric in Excel narrowed universe from 11,000 to top 500 to a final top 100 list.

Tooling

  • Microsoft Excel for modeling, filtering, and scoring.
  • Thermal load visualizations for each shortlisted facility.
  • Sector lens on pulp, fertilizer, chemical, and paper to focus outreach.

Key Findings

Regional Signals

  • Western Australia: 400.06 MW thermal load across shortlisted sites.
  • Victoria: 108.28 MW, strong follow-on region.
  • Germany: 305.70 MW across 9 sites — flagged for limited diversification.

Site-Level Highlights

  • Metsä Board (Finland): >1,000 MW thermal load, standout candidate.
  • SCA Östrand (Sweden): +40 MW increase over 5 years, showing rising intensity.

Impact

Outcome

  • Analyzed 11,000+ facilities to identify $2B+ market opportunities across Europe and Australia.
  • Delivered a prioritized top-100 list with supporting visuals and rubric scores.
  • Enabled data-backed sequencing of sales/partnership outreach by region and sector.

Delivery & Communication

Cadence

  • Weekly milestones with demos of updated dashboards and rubrics.
  • Risk surfacing early when data gaps appeared; captured decisions in short memos.

Artifacts

  • Filterable Excel workbook with scoring logic documented inline.
  • Thermal load charts per site to support exec briefings.
  • Region playbooks summarizing incentives and constraints.
